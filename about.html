PALM OIL PROCESSING

AEN Palm Oil Processing (Pvt.) Ltd.

This is a joint venture of Agalawatta Plantations, Elpitiya Plantations & Namunukula Plantations. The project was set up to process the palm fruits grown in their estates and to extract crude palm oil. The factory located at Mohamaddi Estate, Baduraliya, which has the processing capacity of 10,000 fresh fruits bunches per hour. This is a BOI approved project and the produce is exported to India. The mill was commissioned in January 2007.
